About Me

Hey there! I am Janne Arkko, a professional firefighter, a husband and a father of two beautiful kids, Oliver and Kerttu. If i'm not extinguishing fires and saving people or boxing you can find me playing with my kids in the backyard.


I'm currently participating in a course of selection to become a ICT Engineer. I am thrilled for this opportunity and trying my very best to be elected. Our goal is to create our own website.


Learning new things and exploring the world is what carries me on. Helping the citizens and getting my hands dirty doing so is what motivates me. And the silent respect towards the firefighters all over the world is what rewards me.


ICT is needed in every, even the smallest of, gadgets and components. It really is the business of the future, hence why i am very motivated in learning the field and doing my best in the course of selection.
